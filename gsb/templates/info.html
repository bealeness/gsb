Information

A guide to using GSB

1. MyPersonal

The MyPersonal page is your platform to your GSB Cash account. Your cash balance and cash account number are displayed at top. The Funds Sent and Funds Received windows record all of your outgoing and incoming transactions respectively. For more information regarding each transaction, click on the details link.

2. PaySomeone

The PaySomeone page is where you can send money to another GSB account holder. Don't someones account number? Send them a message in the GSB Social page.

3. MyTerm

The MyTerm page is your platform to your GSB Term Deposit account. When you deposit cash into a term deposit the term deposit name and your balance in that deposit will be displayed at the top of the page. On depositing cash your deposit transaction will be recorded in the Funds Deposited window. On reception of your funds at the maturity of each term deposit, your return transaction will be recorded in the Funds Returned window. For more information on each transaction click on the details link.

4. TermProducts

The TermProducts page is your platform to our available term deposit products. The maturity length is denoted in days. So a maturity of 7, means that your deposit will be kept for seven days. At the conclusion of which you will be returned your balance, with the addition of the stipulated rate. So if the rate is 5% and you deposit $10,000, you will be returned $10,500 at the conclusion of the stipulated maturity period. Click on the details link for more information about each product. To make a deposit, simply choose the product, enter the amount and click on deposit. Be sure to deposit the minimum, or more than the minimum required for each product.

5. MyBond

The MyBond page is your platform to your GSB Bond account. When you purchase

and own bonds your bond balance is displayed at the top of the page. The Your Bonds window tells you how much of each particular bond you own. When you place orders to either buy or sell bonds your order information is displayed in the Your Buy Orders and Your Sell Orders windows respectively. Your order information will remain until they have either been executed, or until the end of trading day when all buy and sell orders will be cleared. If you execute a buy order then your transaction will be recorded in the Purchase Transaction window. If you execute a sell order, then your transaction will be recorded in the Sale Transaction window.

6. BondMarket:BUY

The Buy page is your portal to the buy side of the GSB Bond Market. Any sell orders in the marketplace are displayed at the top of the page. If the Seller ID is 1, then you're dealing with GSB Bank. Because they are always first to sell a bond into the marketplace their offer will always be the bond face value of $1,000. The Bond ID is the number particular to each bond. The quantity is the number of each bond the seller has to offer. The yield is the total return on each bond the bond owner will receive at items maturity. But remember that unlike term deposits, bonds can be traded, which means their prices will vary over their lifetime and that means that the yield, or the return, of a bond will increase or decrease depending on the price paid for it. When the bank initiates the sale of a bond its yield will reflect its underlying coupon rate, which is the return rate at face value.

6. BondMarket:SELL

The Sell page is your portal to the sell side of the GSB Bond Market. Any buy orders in the marketplace are displayed at the top of the page. In this instance GSB Bank will never be present, as it does not buy its own bonds after issuance. But just like the Buy page, all other details are very similar for the Sell page.

7. BondMarket:MARKET

The MARKET page is your portal to the overall state of the GSB Bond Market. The Bonds in market window displays all bonds currently in the marketplace.

8. GSBSocial

The GSB Social page is what makes GSB Bank stand out from the rest. Not only do

we want to offer our customers fantastic banking and financial services, but we also provide a social media service. Here you can view live updates of our leaderboard, share a thought with other GSB Bank users or if you would rather interact privately, then send a message direct to another users inbox. If you wish to correspond directly with GSB Bank, then simply state 'Attn: GSB Bank' into the share something form, followed by your message. We will reply directly to your inbox as soon as we can. We are here to help and serve your every need!

9. AccountSettings

If you would like to update your account details then do so on the AccountSettings page. Additionally, we also provide you with a choice of background images from famous locations in everyone's favourite town in New Zealand - Gisborne. Sunrise at Midway Beach is the default and the most stunning, but if you feel you would enjoy being in Town, then make the change. Gladstone Road is just a click away. If, on the other hand, you prefer the riverside and the picturesque inner harbour, then why not choose Bomb Bridge.
